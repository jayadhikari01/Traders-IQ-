Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect essential information required to maintain your trading terminal. This includes your email address via Firebase Authentication for identity verification and the trade-specific data (PnL, strategy notes, and timestamps) that you manually input into your journal.

2. Payment & Financial Security

Your financial privacy is our priority. All transactions are handled securely by Lemon Squeezy. Traders IQ never sees, stores, or processes your credit card numbers, CVVs, or bank account details. We only receive a confirmation of payment to activate your Elite access.

3. Data Security (Firebase)

All your trading history is stored using industry-standard encryption on Google Firebase Cloud. Your data is private and linked strictly to your unique User ID (UID). We implement strict security rules to ensure that only you can read or write to your personal database.

4. Third-Party Policy

Traders IQ follows a strict "Zero-Share" policy. We do not sell or trade your trading performance or journal entries with third-party marketers, hedge funds, or brokerage firms. Your edge is your own.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated trade data. Once deleted, your logs cannot be recovered from our cloud servers.
